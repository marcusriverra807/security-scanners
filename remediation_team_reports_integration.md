# Integration of Remediation Team Reports into Evidence Collection Pipeline

## Objective
Integrate the weekly remediation team status reports and progress updates into the evidence collection pipeline to enhance visibility and validation of remediation activities for critical and high-severity vulnerabilities.

## Approach
1. Define inputs for remediation team reports in the pipeline.
2. Schedule automated ingestion of weekly status reports.
3. Establish validation checks to ensure timely and accurate reporting.
4. Prioritize evidence collection efforts based on remediation progress and criticality.

## Benefits
- Improved tracking of remediation activities.
- Enhanced evidence validation through structured reporting.
- Better prioritization of pipeline resources on critical vulnerabilities.

## Next Steps
- Collaborate with remediation teams to standardize report formats.
- Implement data ingestion and validation mechanisms.
- Monitor and adjust pipeline integration based on feedback.
